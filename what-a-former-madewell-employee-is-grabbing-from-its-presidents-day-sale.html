The Harlow Wide-Leg Pant

This is me — aka someone who owns Madewell’s Harlow Wide-Leg Pants and wears them, at minimum, once a week — urging you to grab the best-selling trousers while they’re 63 percent off. The style is flattering, lightweight, easy to dress up and down, and available in 11 shades — eight of which are on sale. Plus, they’re a favorite among shoppers, one of which called them “a tailored, classic, wide-legged dream.” “They provide comfort without overwhelming the frame…[and] nip in the waist beautifully,” they added, deeming the style “unquestionably flattering.” And per another customer, the trousers give you a “perfectly snacheted, high waist,” adding that the “gorgeous drape” of the pleats flatters their “curvy” figure.

Angled-Neck Long-Sleeve Top

As you transition your wardrobe from winter to spring, a lightweight long-sleeve top is a must, and I’m personally loving this updated take on the classic style. According to shoppers, the top’s square, angled neckline “makes this must-need basic more elevated.” Like your favorite tee, customers note that “you can style this with jeans for a casual look,” but thanks to that unique neckline, you can just as easily “dress it up with a skirt.” And while untraditional cuts can be finicky when it comes to what bra to wear underneath, fans note that this hides their straps perfectly.

Denim Popover Mini Shirtdress

TBH, I plan to live in the brand’s Denim Popover Mini Shirtdress through the end of spring, styling it with knee-high boots and tights now and simple, chic loafers when the weather starts to warm up. “Everything about this is perfect: fabric, color, and style,” gushed one fan who loves the versatility of the dress, adding that it’s “something that can be worn any season, both dressy and casual.” “You can wear it everywhere,” said another shopper, while someone else noted that the denim is “soft and comfortable to wear,” despite not having a ton of stretch.

The Puff Crossbody Bag

One of the best deals from the sale-on-sale is on this woven leather crossbody bag, now 67 percent off in the shade Chocolate Raisin, which one person described as “a lovely dark brown with a hint of plum.” According to that fan, the size of the bag is perfect, writing that it works as an everyday purse but is small enough to be “tucked in one of Madewell’s totes for traveling.” “[It’s a] great size for everyday use,” said another customer, explaining that it “fits a small wallet, sunglasses, and a few extras.” Plus, they added, the woven style is “unique but [still] pretty much goes with everything.”
