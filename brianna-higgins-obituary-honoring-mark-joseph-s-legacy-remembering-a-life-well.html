Funeral Service Details

Date, Time, and Location

The memorial ceremony honoring the life of Mark Joseph will take place on Friday, September 22, at 10:30 AM. The service will be held at Peace Lutheran Church in Columbus, Nebraska. This sacred space will provide a comforting and serene environment for friends, family, and members of the community to come together and pay their respects to Mark Joseph.

Visitation and Prayer Sessions

In the hours leading up to the funeral service, there will be visitation sessions held to offer an opportunity for loved ones to say their final goodbyes. On Thursday, visitation will be held at the Gass Haney Funeral Home from five o’clock until seven o’clock in the evening. On Friday, visitation will continue at Peace Lutheran Church starting at 9:30 AM and will continue until the time of the service.

Additionally, a prayer session for families will be held at the church on Friday at 10:15 in the morning. This session will provide a moment of solace and reflection, allowing those in attendance to find comfort in their shared grief and offer support to one another during this challenging time.

The funeral service and visitation sessions serve as a way for the community to come together, express their sorrow, and honor the life and legacy of Mark Joseph. It is a time to share memories, offer condolences, and find strength in the collective support of those who knew and loved him.
