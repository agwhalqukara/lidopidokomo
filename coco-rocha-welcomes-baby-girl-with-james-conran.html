Coco Rocha welcomes baby girl with James Conran

 Posted on July 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  443 words  |  Martina Birk

    Coco Rocha and her husband James Conran are now parents to a baby girl. The 26-year-old Canadian model gave birth to her first child on 28 March and announced the news of her little arrival on Instagram."James and I are so proud to welcome into the world our healthy and beautiful baby girl, Ioni James Conran!" she wrote alongside a photo of her baby. "She's the best of both of us and completely perfect from head to toe. #BabyConran is now officially @ioniconran!"

    Baby Ioni made her way into the world on 28 March

    Ioni's new Instagram account, which has already posted several pictures documenting her early days, bears the description. "Formerly known as #babyconran, they call me Ioni now."British artist James echoed his wife's excitement on Twitter, posting a photo of the little family that showed himself and Coco gazzingly lovingly at their bundle of joy."And then 2 became 3!" he captioned the photo. "Coco and I are so excited to be joined by our sweet baby girl, Ioni James Conran. #IoniConran."

    "Just a few hours old and I'm already a master of emoting. Here I present just 4 from my repertoire: "doubting", "bored" "suspicious" and "intrigued"."

    Both Coco and James, who tied the knot in 2010, are clearly besotted with their newborn daughter."There is nothing more surreal and humbling than to look into the face of your baby," tweeted James. "One thing I didn't think I'd be doing his weekend? A tiny baby manicure #NailsLikeTalons #IoniConran. "Toronto-born Coco admitted she also couldn't stop looking at her daughter either, writing: "#IoniConran is growing up so fast. Barely a day old and so alert and expressive! Giving me @GigiHadid vibes here."Gigi herself was swift to send the new parents her best wishes. "Congratulations @cocorocha @jamesconran!!" tweeted the American model. "So happy for you, & sending all my love to the precious Conran fam!"

    Little Ioni is Coco and James' first child together

    In February Coco revealed that she and James were expecting a baby girl, writing: "So excited to have had a glimpse of my baby girl this afternoon!! That's right, #BabyConran is a little lady! Mommy and daddy could not be happier."Coco wed her "best friend" James in the Loire Valley in France and the couple celebrated their fourth anniversary on 9 June 2014."I'm not sure what I would ever do without you," she said on their special day. "Thank you for making my life meaningful."
