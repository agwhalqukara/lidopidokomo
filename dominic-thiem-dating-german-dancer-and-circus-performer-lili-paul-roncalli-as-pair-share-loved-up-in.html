TENNIS ace Dominic Thiem has revealed he is dating German dancer and circus performer Lili-Paul Roncalli.

The pair shared loved up pictures on Instagram as they announced to the world their relationship.

Lili-Paul is a German circus performer and is also renowned for her ability as a dancer.

The contortionist won the Let's Dance reality show in May 2020.

Thiem and Lili-Paul have been speculated to be dating for months but their Instagram poses have put the rumours to bed.

Thiem won his first Grand Slam title after a stunning comeback win against Alex Zverev to land the US Open last September.

The 27-year-old Austrian is ranked third in the world and will be among the favourites to win the Australian Open which gets underway next month.
