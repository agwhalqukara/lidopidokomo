What is Kwame Holland's Net Worth?

Kwame Holland is an American emcee and producer who has a net worth of $500 thousand dollars. Born in 1973, Kwame Holland experienced most of his fame during the late '80s and early '90s. He made his music debut with the 1989 album "Kwame the Boy Genius Featuring a New Beginning". He released several singles off the album, including one with an accompanying music video that featured a ton of polka dots. The polka dots soon went on to become synonymous with Kwame, which inspired a polka-dot fashion trend. He released his follow-up album, "A Day in the Life: A Pokadelick Adventure" in 1990 and his third album, "Nastee", in 1992.

His fourth effort, "Incognito", in 1994, did not chart, and Kwame disappeared from the music scene until 2000. He resurfaced as a producer, working with a handful of artists, including Missy Elliott, LL Cool J, Mary J. Blige, and Christina Aguilera. He co-produced "On Fire" with Eminem in 2004 and worked on "Switch" for Will Smith in 2005. Kwame has sold more than 30 million albums as a producer. He also composes music for movies and TV shows, working on "Fantastic Four", "Freedom Writers", "Coach Carter", "Drumline", and the "Step Up" franchise.
